Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive? +

Please arrive at least 30 minutes before the ceremony begins at 4:00 PM to allow time for seating.

Is there parking available? +

Yes, both venues have ample parking space available for guests. Valet service will also be provided.

Can I bring a plus one? +

Please refer to your invitation. If a plus one is included, it will be indicated on your RSVP.

Are children welcome? +

We love your little ones, but we've chosen to make our wedding day an adult-only celebration. We hope this allows you to relax and enjoy the evening.

What should I wear? +

Formal Scottish attire is requested. Traditional Scottish dress is encouraged. Please see our Dress Code section for more details.

Will there be food and drinks? +

Yes! A full dinner reception with Scottish and international cuisine will be served, along with an open bar featuring premium beverages.

When is the RSVP deadline? +

Please RSVP by April 18, 2026, to help us finalize arrangements with our vendors.

We kindly request an unplugged ceremony. Please refrain from taking photos during the ceremony. Feel free to capture memories during the reception!
